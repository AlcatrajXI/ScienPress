  • Q2. Is there a print version of the Scientific Press International Limited journal?

    Yes, Scientific Press International Limited published both online and print version.

  • Q3. How do I submit my paper to a Scientific Press International Limited journal?

    All papers must be submitted through our online submission system. In case you might have problems to submit the paper electronically, please send it via e-mail. (Visit Author Guidelines)

  • Q4. How long does the review process take?

    The papers will be reviewed by two independent referees. The review will be send to you within 3-5 weeks after submission. Do not hesitate to contact us if you would like to know the status of your manuscript.

  • Q5. What type of file format do you accept?

    Papers in Word or PDF is the format for submitting your paper to our online submission system. After the acceptance of your paper, you can send your revised paper in MS Word or LaTex via email to us.
